We hereby inform you that said discount is applicable only for insured adults from 18 Years and above. Dependent child/ children under this policy do not qualify for this discount.

Please note-
  • •This is a one-time assessment which can be availed only at the time of purchase, not after policy issuance (if issued)

  • •All eligible members covered under this plan must be together and must take this assessment one after the other
  • •In case of any misrepresentation during the scan, discount availed will be recovered from your policy renewal premium
